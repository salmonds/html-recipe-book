Discount Spaghetti

Submitted by Al

There is only one thing worse than the discount spaghetti and it is hell itself.

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen
  • Optional ramen flavor packet
  • Apathy
  • Water
  • Ketchup packets

Instructions

  1. ...You dont really want me to spell this out, do you?
  2. Fine, whatever. Put a pot of water on the stove and heat to a boil.
  3. Put in the slab of solid raman. Optional flavor packet too, if you wish.
  4. When noodles are soft, grab the hot noodles with your bare hand and pull it out onto the counter.
  5. Apply 1-5 packets of ketchup stolen from Mcdonalds.
  6. Weep.
