Default Sandwich

Submitted by Al Garza

Fun fact about the "Default Sandwich", is is actually a pb&j sandwich in a funny costume. Also, I drew the breathtaking graphic myself.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Jelly (type of your choice)
  • Peanut Butter
  • A healthy attitude and a heart open to divine sandwich grace

Instructions

  1. Grab two slices of bread and lay them open in front of you.
  2. On one slice, evenly spread a layer of peanut butter.
  3. On the other slice, evenly spread a layer of jelly.
  4. Line up both slices of bread and press the spread faces against eachother.
  5. Admire the default sandwich.
  6. Sob in the presence of such beauty.
